Who is this app for?


The MuseScore Player app is designed for anyone who wants to learn faster or has a hard time reading sheet music. The MuseScore Player will let you listen to the notes at your chosen tempo, as well as change the volume of the other parts. These features make it much easier to learn those difficult passages and speed up your ability to perfect your part!
